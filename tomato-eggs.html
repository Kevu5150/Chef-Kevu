Mains Eggs 20 min

Tomato & Eggs

A lot of you guys have been saying my past recipes are way too long, so this time I got something short and sweet. Tomato and egg is a quick, simple, and easy comfort dish to make. The tomatoes get super soft and saucy, while the eggs soak up the flavor. Combined with rice, this is a meal that is easy, but still fills you up.

20 min
Total Time
5 min
Prep
2
Servings
Easy
Difficulty
Ingredients

Main

Tomatoes2 medium
Eggs3
Chicken stock1/2 cup
Sugar1 tsp
Saltto taste
Oil (for eggs)1 tsp
Oil (for wok)1 tbsp
Instructions
The Prep
1

Cut the tomatoes

Cut the tomatoes into medium-sized wedges and set them aside.

2

Beat the eggs

Crack 3 eggs into a bowl and beat them until smooth. Add a small pinch of salt.

The Eggs
3

Scramble the eggs

Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the eggs and gently scramble them until just cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.

💡 Don't fully cook the eggs, they will finish cooking when you put them back with the tomatoes.
The Tomatoes
4

Cook the tomatoes

Add 1 tablespoon of oil to a wok or pan over medium heat. Add the tomato wedges and stir for about 1 minute.

5

Simmer

Pour in 1/2 cup of chicken stock. Cover with a lid and let the tomatoes simmer for about 8 minutes, or until they become soft.

6

Break down the tomatoes

Use a spoon to gently press and break apart the tomatoes so they release more juice into the sauce.

Finishing
7

Add the eggs back in

Add the scrambled eggs back into the wok. Sprinkle in 1 teaspoon of sugar and mix everything together.

8

Final minute

Let it cook for about 1 more minute so the eggs soak up the tomato sauce.

9

Serve

Serve warm with rice.
